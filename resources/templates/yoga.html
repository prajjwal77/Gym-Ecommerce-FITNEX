About Yoga

Yoga is a spiritual and physical practice that emphasizes breathing techniques, poses, and meditation to promote health, wellness, and inner peace.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility and balance
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Boosts mental clarity and focus
  • Strengthens muscles and joints
